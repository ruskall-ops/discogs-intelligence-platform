# Release Intelligence

> **Helping collectors discover tomorrow's collectables before the market catches up.**

---

# Vision

Release Intelligence is a future module of the Discogs Intelligence Platform designed to identify newly announced and recently released records that exhibit characteristics historically associated with future collector demand.

Rather than analysing established marketplace activity, Release Intelligence focuses on the period **before** or **immediately after release**, when opportunities are most likely to be overlooked.

Its purpose is not to predict future prices.

Its purpose is to identify releases that deserve further investigation.

The collector always makes the final decision.

---

# The Problem

Many of the most desirable modern records become difficult or expensive to obtain shortly after release.

Collectors frequently discover these records only after:

- the initial pressing has sold out;
- listings appear on Discogs at significantly higher prices;
- secondary market demand has already increased.

A common response from collectors is:

> "I wish I'd known about that release earlier."

Release Intelligence exists to reduce those missed opportunities.

---

# Mission

Help collectors discover releases that may become highly desirable by analysing objective signals before those signals become widely recognised.

The platform never recommends purchasing.

It simply highlights releases that deserve closer attention.

---

# Product Philosophy

Release Intelligence follows the core philosophy of DIP.

> **Automate the research, not the collecting decision.**

The platform provides evidence.

The collector provides judgement.

---

# Typical Questions

Release Intelligence aims to answer questions such as:

- Which new releases deserve my attention?
- Which limited pressings are already selling unusually quickly?
- Which producers are currently generating exceptional collector interest?
- Which labels consistently release sought-after records?
- Which releases match my collecting interests?
- Which releases may become difficult to obtain?
- Which releases are generating unusually strong early signals?

# Signal Engine

The Signal Engine combines independent indicators.

No individual signal should trigger a recommendation.

Instead the platform looks for combinations.

Example:

✓ Limited pressing

✓ One per customer

✓ Growing producer

✓ DJ support

✓ Rapid retailer sell-out

✓ High community discussion

Together these provide significantly stronger evidence.

# Relationship with Market Discovery

Release Intelligence operates before significant marketplace history exists.

Once sufficient historical data becomes available, releases transition into Market Discovery.

Release Intelligence answers:

> What deserves attention before everyone notices?

Market Discovery answers:

> What is happening across the market today?

Together they provide continuous intelligence throughout a record's lifecycle.

---

# Success

Success is not measured by accurately predicting future prices.

Success is measured by consistently identifying releases that collectors later wish they had known about sooner.
