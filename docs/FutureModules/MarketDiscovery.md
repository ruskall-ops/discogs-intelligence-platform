# Market Discovery

> **Finding tomorrow's opportunities before they become obvious.**

---

# Vision

Market Discovery extends the Discogs Intelligence Platform beyond analysing the user's own collection.

Its purpose is to continuously analyse the wider vinyl market and identify records that deserve further investigation before they become widely recognised.

The platform does **not** recommend purchases.

Instead it identifies emerging opportunities supported by transparent evidence and allows the collector to decide whether further research is worthwhile.

This module follows the core DIP philosophy:

> **Automate the research, not the collecting decision.**

---

## Relationship to Release Intelligence

Market Discovery analyses records that have entered the wider marketplace and have sufficient historical data for meaningful analysis.

It follows Release Intelligence within the collector journey.

Release Intelligence identifies early opportunities before significant market history exists.

Market Discovery analyses how those opportunities evolve over time.

Together they provide continuous intelligence throughout the lifecycle of a record.

---

# Why This Module Exists

Most collectors only discover records after prices have already increased.

Market Discovery aims to surface signals much earlier by combining marketplace data with wider indicators of demand, popularity and cultural momentum.

The objective is not to predict the future with certainty.

The objective is to identify records exhibiting characteristics that historically preceded increased collector interest.

---

# Target Users

Market Discovery is designed for:

- Serious collectors
- Vinyl investors
- DJs
- Record dealers
- Music enthusiasts
- Long-term collectors

It complements rather than replaces traditional collecting.

---

# Core Philosophy

The platform should never say:

> Buy this record.

Instead it should say:

> This record has exhibited several emerging market signals that may justify further investigation.

The final decision always belongs to the collector.

# Signal Engine

The Signal Engine combines multiple independent indicators.

Examples:

✓ Increasing Wants

✓ Decreasing Supply

✓ Stable Prices

✓ Increasing Search Volume

✓ Social Media Activity

✓ DJ Support

✓ Chart Appearances

✓ Reissue Announcements

✓ Label Momentum

✓ Genre Momentum

One signal alone is rarely meaningful.

Multiple independent signals increase confidence.

---

# Market Discovery Score

A future Market Discovery Score may combine factors such as:

- Demand
- Supply
- Scarcity
- Momentum
- Social Activity
- Market Confidence

The weighting should remain transparent and configurable.

---

# Confidence Score

Every recommendation should include a confidence score.

Confidence reflects the quality and consistency of the supporting evidence rather than the likelihood of future price appreciation.

---

# Historical Validation

Every generated opportunity should be stored.

This allows DIP to answer questions such as:

- Which signals historically proved useful?
- Which indicators produced the strongest results?
- Which combinations generated the most successful discoveries?

The platform should continuously learn from historical outcomes without becoming a black-box prediction engine.
