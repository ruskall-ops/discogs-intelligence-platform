## Business Model

The platform follows a freemium intelligence model.

Free users receive meaningful value through:

- Collection intelligence
- Historical tracking
- Basic reporting
- Dashboard analytics
- Limited watchlists

Premium users unlock:

- Advanced opportunity scoring
- AI-assisted intelligence
- Unlimited watchlists
- Portfolio analytics
- Advanced reports
- Alerts
- Faster refresh schedules
- Marketplace intelligence
- Platform intelligence

As adoption grows, anonymised aggregate data continuously improves the quality of insights available to every user, creating a positive feedback loop between community growth and platform intelligence.
