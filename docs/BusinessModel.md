# Business Model

---

## Purpose

The purpose of the business model is not simply to generate revenue.

Its purpose is to ensure the long-term sustainability of the Discogs Intelligence Platform, allowing continuous development, improvements and support for the collecting community.

Revenue enables the platform to evolve.

The product comes first.

---

# Product Philosophy

DIP is built around one guiding principle:

> Automate the research, not the collecting decision.

Users pay for better intelligence, not for access to their own data.

---

# Core Principles

## Value before monetisation

The platform must become genuinely valuable before asking users to subscribe.

Every paid feature should solve a meaningful problem.

---

## Intelligence over limitations

Subscription tiers should unlock intelligence rather than restrict ownership.

Collectors should never feel punished for growing their collection.

Instead, higher tiers should provide deeper analysis, richer insights and additional automation.

---

## Fair Pricing

Pricing should feel fair relative to the value provided.

The aim is long-term relationships rather than short-term revenue.

---

## Continuous Improvement

Subscribers are investing in the continued development of the platform.

Every release should improve the product.

---

# Potential Subscription Model

This is an early concept and may evolve over time.

## Free

Designed for casual collectors.

Potential features:

- Collection import
- Basic valuation
- Limited analytics
- Basic reporting

---

## Collector

Designed for enthusiasts.

Potential additions:

- Historical snapshots
- Opportunity Engine
- Hidden Gems
- Weekly Intelligence Reports
- Dashboard
- Advanced filtering

---

## Collector Pro

Designed for serious collectors and dealers.

Potential additions:

- Unlimited historical analysis
- Multi-market intelligence
- Advanced reporting
- AI-assisted explanations
- Price alerts
- Collection performance analytics
- API integrations

---

# Long-Term Opportunities

Potential future revenue streams may include:

- Subscription plans
- Dealer tools
- Marketplace integrations
- Mobile companion app
- Cloud synchronisation
- Premium AI features
- Data insights (using anonymised, aggregated, opt-in data only)

---

# What DIP Will Never Become

The platform should never:

- Become advertising driven
- Sell user data
- Prioritise monetisation over user value
- Lock users into proprietary formats
- Restrict ownership of their own collection

Users should always be able to export their own data.

---

# Success

The success of DIP should not be measured solely by revenue.

Success means building a product that collectors genuinely enjoy using and trust to help them make better collecting decisions.

Revenue is the outcome of delivering that value consistently.

Users should feel they are paying because they want to support a product they love, not because they are forced to unlock basic functionality.
